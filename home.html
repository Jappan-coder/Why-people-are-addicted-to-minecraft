About Minecraft

Minecraft is a sandbox video game that allows players to build and explore virtual worlds made out of blocks. It was first released in 2011 and has since become one of the most popular video games of all time.

Addiction Signs

  • Playing for hours without taking a break
  • Thinking about the game constantly
  • Difficulty stopping playing even when it's time to do other things
  • Neglecting personal hygiene, social life, or responsibilities due to the game

How to Control

There are several things you can do to control your Minecraft addiction:

  1. Set limits on how much time you spend playing
  2. Take regular breaks and stretch your legs
  3. Engage in other activities that you enjoy
  4. Talk to friends and family about your gaming habits
